FLAGSHIP EVENTS

01 HACK-OFF

‘Hack Off’, a one of a kind hack, where your innovation defines you. Hack Off is inviting young minds from all across the nation, to come together to put up the greatest show the digital realm has ever seen. Hack Off is not just a hackathon, its a celebration, complete with fun events, games, and exciting prizes!

02VITTUC

VITTUC is a two day National Conference organised annually by the UK based chapter in VIT, IET-onCampus VIT.The Conference that has been attracting more than 300 participants every year and has never failed to surpass their expectations.

03PATW

Present Around The World is the Institution of Engineering and Technology's global competition for Young Professionals and Students within engineering to develop and showcase presentation skills.

04ALPHA SERIES

Alpha series is a series of free workshops being organised at regular intervals,covering a number of technical topics from different engineering branches.

05BETA SERIES

Beta series, the brand new series by IET-VIT, incorporates talk sessions being organised at regular intervals,covering future opportunities for various engineering branches as well as the skill sets required to build your profile.
